Highlights (Primary Care Diabetes – MANDATORY, max 85 characters per bullet)

• Ecological study of 47 Japanese prefectures examined unemployment and diabetes.
• Unemployment rate showed no association with HbA1c ≥ 6.5% (r = −0.016, p = 0.916).
• Null findings were robust across all six sensitivity analysis specifications.
• Per-capita income was a strong inverse predictor (r = −0.570, p < 0.001).
• Social determinants of diabetes may not transfer across socioeconomic contexts.
